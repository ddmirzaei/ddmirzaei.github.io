Short Research Presentation

My research interests lie in developing, implementing, and analyzing numerical methods for function approximation and numerical solution of partial differential equations (PDEs). I have primarily focused on the scattered data approximation methods, which employ techniques based on unorganized points rather than predefined meshes. In this subject, I have followed and developed methods based on moving least squares (MLS) approximations and radial basis function (RBF) interpolations.
